About Us

We are a community of developers and software engineers who strive to find new ways for people to connect and learn from eachother. Our main goal has always been to bring the human connection to every product we create. Meet the team!

Welcome Home

Welcome to Advice Giver. Yeah, we're an app. But we aren't just an app. We are friends, we are family, we are community. A place that is safe. A place with real people, going through real life stuff. A place that focuses on growth and friendship. A place where you can get advice (or give advice!) and have a meaningful impact in someone else's life. Our goal with technology has always been to connect people from around the world, but also promote minimal screen time.
